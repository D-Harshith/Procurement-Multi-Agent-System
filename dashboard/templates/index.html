Starbucks Procurement Multi-Agent System

A multi-agent system for optimizing Starbucks coffee bean procurement

Launch Interactive Dashboard

Coffee Bean Sourcing Agent

Identifies and evaluates potential coffee bean suppliers worldwide based on quality, sustainability, reliability, and price. Continuously monitors global coffee markets and production to find the best sources for Starbucks' high-quality beans.

  • Evaluates supplier quality and reliability
  • Monitors global coffee markets
  • Identifies new sourcing opportunities
  • Tracks climate and political factors

Contract Negotiation Agent

Handles supplier negotiations and contract management to secure the most favorable terms for Starbucks. Analyzes historical pricing data, negotiates volume discounts, and balances cost savings with relationship maintenance.

  • Analyzes historical pricing data
  • Negotiates volume discounts
  • Manages contract renewals
  • Implements negotiation strategies

Order Management Agent

Manages the actual purchasing process and order tracking to ensure timely and efficient procurement of coffee beans. Generates purchase orders, tracks order status, and handles exceptions to maintain a continuous supply chain.

  • Generates purchase orders
  • Tracks order status
  • Handles exceptions and delays
  • Coordinates with logistics

System Architecture

Agent Communication

Agents communicate through a central message broker using a standardized protocol. Messages include requests, responses, notifications, and commands with varying priority levels. This allows for coordinated decision-making and information sharing.

Simulation Environment

The system includes a simulation environment that models coffee market conditions, supplier behavior, and supply chain dynamics. This allows agents to make decisions in a realistic context and adapt to changing conditions.
